TouristDestination

Defined in the pending.schema.org extension.
Canonical URL: http://schema.org/TouristDestination

Thing > Place > TouristDestination

A tourist destination. In principle any Place can be a TouristDestination from a City, Region or Country to an AmusementPark or Hotel. This Type can be used on its own to describe a general TourstDestination, or be used as an additionalType to add tourist relevant properties to any other Place. A TouristDestination is defined as a Place that contains, or is colocated with, one or more TourstAttractions, often linked by a similar theme or interest to a particular touristType. The UNWTO defines Destination (main destination of a tourism trip) as the place visited that is central to the decision to take the trip. (See examples below).
