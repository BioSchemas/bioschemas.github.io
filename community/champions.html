Bioschemas Champions

Bioschemas champions are advocates of Bioschemas in their communities. Champions proactively promote the adoption of Bioschemas and help with questions and mark-up development. Champions can represent a community, a region, or working group, or some other collection of people and resources. Please see the list of champions below to find one near to you or working in your community or region. Our new Champions Club gives support to these important advocates.

If you would like to become a champion, then please contact the Steering Council.
