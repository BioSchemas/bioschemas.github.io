Bioschemas Champions

Bioschemas champions are community representatives who are available to help with initial questions about Bioschemas and developing markup. Champions can represent a working group, community, or node. Please see the list of champions below to find one near to you or working in your community or node.

If you fancy becoming a community champion, then please contact the Steering Council.
