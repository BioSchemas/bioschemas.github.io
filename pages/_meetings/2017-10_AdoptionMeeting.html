This meeting will

  1. Demonstrate through examples the usage of the Bioschemas specifications
  2. Develop markup for a wider set of resources
  3. Identify revisions to the Bioschema specifications to enable their uptake for a wide range of biological resources.

The ELIXIR Bioschemas project has 4 stages: planning, agreement, adoption and application. The topic of this third meeting is adoption. We expect data resources especially those part of the project to adopt and implement Bioschemas compliant markup. The main bioschemas types developed during this implementation study to be considered for adoption will be Data repository, Datasets, Biological Entry, Beacons, Protein, Samples, and Phenotypes. We welcome data resources, especially external data resources, willing to evaluate during the meeting the adoption and implementation of bioschemas markup. We also welcome other groups in Bioschemas willing to push adoption of other content types like Tools, Events, and Training materials.

Full details of the meeting are available in the agenda document, including logistic details. The agenda document will be used to capture notes from the meeting.
