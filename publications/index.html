How to cite Bioschemas

To cite Bioschemas please use the website URL, https://bioschemas.org and "Gray, A.J.G, Goble, C.A. and Jimenez, R., 2017. Bioschemas: From Potato Salad to Protein Annotation. In International Semantic Web Conference (Posters, Demos & Industry Tracks)."
