Note that SDTT is being deprecated and you will receive a warning message about this.

Please remember that Google sets minimum requirements for Schema.org markup, which may differ with those of Bioscheams. As such, Google's SDTT may provide warnings or errors when the markup is perfectly compliant with both Schema.org and Bioscheams.

If your Bioschemas compliant site is not listed below, please email us at enquiries@bioschemas.org.
